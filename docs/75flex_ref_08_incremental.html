Accessed via long-press on the water or steps commitment card on the daily log. No other cards support long-press in MVP.
Large current/goal numbers front and center. Citrus progress bar. Percentage with encouraging copy — never negative.
Quick-add chips for fast logging. Subtract chip in amber — distinct but calm. No negative connotation.
State pill at bottom is always overridable regardless of numeric progress. At 75% water the user can still mark Done — 48oz was enough for them today. "Your call" label reinforces this.
State auto-suggests: 0% = Not Started, 1–99% = Partial, 100% = Done. But user has final say always.
08.1 shows DONE selected (user decided 48oz was enough). 08.2 shows PARTIAL selected (auto-suggested at 73%).
